Frequently Asked Questions

Another common question is whether the units smell in high temperatures?

We use premium deodorizing chemicals and offer increased service frequencies to keep units fresh during hot weather.

Can I rent porta potties for a long-term project?

Yes. We support long-term construction, municipal projects, and maintenance work with scalable servicing plans and scheduled pumping.

Can I move the porta potty once it has been delivered?

We strongly advise against moving the units yourself, as they must be properly leveled and stabilized to prevent tipping. If you need to relocate a unit, please call our team, and we will handle the move professionally to ensure safety.

People also wonder about the height and width of the units for delivery purposes?

Our standard units fit through most standard gate openings, but we can provide exact dimensions for tight spaces.

Do you handle placement and servicing?

Yes. Our team handles placement, pumping, sanitizing, and scheduled servicing.

Can you deliver units on weekends or holidays?

We work closely with our clients to accommodate specific delivery windows, including weekends for special events. We recommend booking in advance to ensure your preferred time slot is available during busy seasons.
